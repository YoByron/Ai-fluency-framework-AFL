# AFL-4 Curriculum: AI Strategy & Leadership (Organizational Transformers)

## Objective
Equip top leaders to set AI vision, design policy, build ecosystems, and embed CACF into strategic decision-making.

## Target Audience
- C-suite, senior policymakers, institutional leaders

## Duration
120–160 hours (executive program; workshops + labs + policy studio)

## Core Competencies
1. Enterprise/sector AI strategy and portfolio governance
2. Policy/regulatory design and public engagement
3. Ecosystem building (industry–academia–government)
4. Strategic CACF oversight and accountability

## Modules
- Vision & Strategy Formulation
- Policy and Regulatory Design
- Ecosystem & Partnerships
- Ethical Leadership & CACF Oversight

## Assessments
- Strategy dossier (3–5 year plan with KPIs and budgets)
- Policy brief/regulatory proposal (stakeholder-mapped)
- Partnership blueprint and sustainability model

## Certification
Certificate: “AI Strategy & Leadership (AFL-4)” — 3-year validity
