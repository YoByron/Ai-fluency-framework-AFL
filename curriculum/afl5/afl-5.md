# AFL-5 Curriculum: AI Thought Leadership & Societal Impact (Global Innovators)

## Objective
Develop leaders who advance research, shape global governance, and drive large-scale societal impact anchored in constitutional values.

## Target Audience
- Senior executives, policymakers, researchers, global fellows

## Duration
200+ hours (largely self-directed; supervised research + field lab)

## Core Competencies
1. Original research and framework development
2. Global governance and diplomacy
3. Societal transformation at scale (grand challenges)
4. Constitutional AI innovation (CACF extensions)

## Deliverables
- Peer-reviewed research or equivalent policy impact
- Large-scale initiative (measurable outcomes)
- CACF extension/design note adopted by institutions

## Certification
Fellowship recognition: “AI Thought Leader (AFL-5)” — periodic impact review
