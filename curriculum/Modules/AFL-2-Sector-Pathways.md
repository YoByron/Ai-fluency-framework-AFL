# AFL-2 Sector Specialization Pathways
## AI Integration for Specific Industries

**Version:** 1.0  
**Duration:** 30 Hours per Sector (Learner chooses 1)  
**Focus:** Process Innovation & Workflow Optimization

---

## 1. Healthcare Integration (30 Hours)
**Target:** Hospital Administrators, Clinical Leads, Public Health Managers

### Module H1: Healthcare Workflow Analysis (8 hours)
- **Patient Flow Optimization:** Mapping patient journey from admission to discharge. Identifying bottlenecks (wait times, triage).
- **Diagnostic Support Workflows:** Integrating AI diagnostic tools (radiology, pathology) into clinical workflows without disrupting care.
- **Administrative Automation:** Automating billing, insurance claims, and appointment scheduling.

### Module H2: CACF in Healthcare (8 hours)
- **Patient Privacy & Data Security:** DISHA compliance, anonymization protocols.
- **Bias in Medical AI:** Detecting demographic bias in diagnostic algorithms.
- **Clinical Safety Protocols:** Human-in-the-loop requirements for life-critical decisions.

### Module H3: Training & Capacity Building (6 hours)
- **Training Clinical Staff:** Teaching doctors and nurses to use AI tools effectively.
- **Patient Education:** Using AI to generate personalized patient education materials in local languages.

### Module H4: Innovation Project (8 hours)
- **Project:** Redesign a specific hospital department workflow (e.g., Emergency Triage) using AI to improve efficiency by 20%.

## 2. Financial Services Integration (30 Hours)
**Target:** Bank Managers, Loan Officers, Fintech Product Leads

### Module F1: Financial Workflow Analysis (8 hours)
- **Loan Processing Automation:** Streamlining document verification and credit scoring.
- **Customer Service Workflows:** Integrating AI chatbots for Tier 1 support and personalized advisory.
- **Fraud Detection Integration:** Real-time transaction monitoring workflows.

### Module F2: CACF in Finance (8 hours)
- **Fair Lending Practices:** Auditing credit algorithms for caste/gender/region bias.
- **Regulatory Compliance:** RBI guidelines on AI/ML usage.
- **Explainability:** Ensuring AI financial decisions can be explained to customers.

### Module F3: Training & Capacity Building (6 hours)
- **Upskilling Branch Staff:** Training employees on new AI-assisted banking tools.
- **Financial Literacy:** AI tools for customer financial education.

### Module F4: Innovation Project (8 hours)
- **Project:** Develop an "AI-Assisted Loan Approval" workflow that reduces processing time from days to hours while maintaining risk standards.

## 3. Education Integration (30 Hours)
**Target:** School Principals, EdTech Managers, University Deans

### Module E1: Educational Workflow Analysis (8 hours)
- **Personalized Learning Pathways:** Integrating AI tutors to adapt curriculum to student needs.
- **Assessment Automation:** AI-assisted grading and feedback workflows.
- **Administrative Efficiency:** Timetable scheduling, attendance tracking, and enrollment automation.

### Module E2: CACF in Education (8 hours)
- **Student Data Privacy:** Protecting minor's data (DPDP Act).
- **Bias in Assessment:** Ensuring grading algorithms are fair across demographics.
- **Age-Appropriate AI:** Safety guardrails for student interaction.

### Module E3: Training & Capacity Building (6 hours)
- **Teacher Training:** Train-the-trainer programs for integrating AI in classrooms.
- **Parental Engagement:** AI tools for communicating student progress to parents.

### Module E4: Innovation Project (8 hours)
- **Project:** Design a "Smart Classroom" workflow where AI assists teachers with lesson planning, grading, and personalized student support.

## 4. Governance Integration (30 Hours)
**Target:** District Collectors, Municipal Commissioners, Policy Analysts

### Module G1: Governance Workflow Analysis (8 hours)
- **Public Service Delivery:** Automating certificate issuance (caste, income, birth).
- **Grievance Redressal:** AI workflows for categorizing and routing citizen complaints.
- **Policy Analysis:** Using AI to analyze public feedback and data for policy making.

### Module G2: CACF in Governance (8 hours)
- **Democratic Values:** Ensuring AI systems serve all citizens equally.
- **Transparency & Accountability:** Public auditability of government AI algorithms.
- **Exclusion Error Mitigation:** Preventing AI from denying benefits to eligible citizens.

### Module G3: Training & Capacity Building (6 hours)
- **Civil Servant Training:** Capacity building for government employees on AI tools.
- **Citizen Awareness:** Programs to help citizens interact with government AI services.

### Module G4: Innovation Project (8 hours)
- **Project:** Create an "AI-Enhanced Public Grievance System" that reduces resolution time by 50% and improves citizen satisfaction.

### Module M4: Innovation Project (8 hours)
