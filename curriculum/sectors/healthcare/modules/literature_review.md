 # Module: Literature Review & Synthesis (Healthcare)
 
 Outcomes: synthesize guidelines and papers into clinician-ready briefs with references.
 
 Activities:
 - Summarize 3 guideline sources into a one-pager with citations.
 - Draft a comparison table of treatments with pros/cons and evidence levels.
 
 Assessment: citation quality, neutrality, and accuracy; CACF high-stakes oversight noted.
