 # Module: Admin Efficiency (Education)
 
 Outcomes: automate routine reporting, timetable drafts, and parent communications to reduce admin load.
 
 Activities:
 - Generate a monthly report (attendance, activities) from a simple CSV; create parent summary letters.
 - Draft a timetable update notice and SMS templates in English + local language.
 
 Assessment: 20%+ time saved (self-reported baseline vs. after), clarity/readability, and disclosure of AI use.
 
 Sample data: ../sample_data/admin_reports_template.csv
