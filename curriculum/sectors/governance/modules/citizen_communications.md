 # Module: Citizen Communications (Governance)
 
 Outcomes: draft RTI/citizen responses, notices, and FAQs in plain language with translation.
 
 Activities:
 - Create a citizen FAQ in English + local language; verify clarity.
 - Draft an RTI-style reply with references to acts/rules.
 
 Assessment: clarity check (readability), translation fidelity, disclosure of AI use.
