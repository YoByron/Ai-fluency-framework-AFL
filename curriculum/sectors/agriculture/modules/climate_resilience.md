 # Module: Climate Resilience Planning (Agriculture)
 
 Outcomes: plan sowing/irrigation buffers and crop choices using seasonal patterns and risk notes.
 
 Activities:
 - Create a seasonal plan (3 months) with irrigation buffers using climate template CSV.
 - Draft risk notes (heat, rainfall variance) and mitigation options.
 
 Assessment: practicality, alignment with local guidance, and multilingual clarity.
