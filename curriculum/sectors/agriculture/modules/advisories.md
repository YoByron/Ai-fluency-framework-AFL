 # Module: Weather/Crop/Price Advisories (Agriculture)
 
 Outcomes: access localized weather, crop calendars, and market prices; formulate actionable advisories.
 
 Activities:
 - Retrieve 7-day weather forecast; plan irrigation schedule.
 - Compile local mandi prices; decide sell/hold with justification.
 
 Assessment: advisory write-up with data references; local language support.
