## 1. BACKGROUND AND CONTEXT

**WHEREAS** the Government of India has launched the AI Fluency Levels (AFL) Framework aligned with Viksit Bharat 2047;

**WHEREAS** the Corporate Partner is committed to Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) under Section 135 of the Companies Act, 2013;

**WHEREAS** AI literacy and skill development are eligible CSR activities under Schedule VII of the Companies Act, 2013;

**WHEREAS** the Constitutional AI Compliance Framework (CACF) ensures equitable, rights-based AI deployment;

**WHEREAS** both Parties recognize the mutual benefits of collaboration to build mass AI fluency;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, the Parties agree to collaborate as follows:

## 11. CONSTITUTIONAL AI COMPLIANCE FRAMEWORK (CACF)

### 11.1 CACF Commitment

Both Parties commit to upholding CACF principles:
- **Article 14 (Equality):** Equal access and service quality
- **Article 15 (Non-Discrimination):** Zero tolerance for bias
- **Article 21 (Life and Liberty):** Privacy protection, explainability
- **Article 21A (Right to Education):** Universal access to AI literacy

### 11.2 Equity Assurance

- Proactive outreach to underserved communities
- Scholarships for economically disadvantaged participants
- Multilingual content and accessible design
- Grievance redress for discrimination

### 11.3 Ethical AI Use

- Corporate Partner's AI tools/platforms to comply with CACF
- Bias audits before deployment
- Transparency in AI decision-making
- Participant consent for data use

## 15. TERM AND TERMINATION

### 15.1 Term

This MOU shall be effective from [Start Date] and shall remain in force until [End Date] or until objectives are achieved, whichever is later.

### 15.2 Extension

This MOU may be extended by mutual written consent, subject to satisfactory performance and CSR Committee approval.

### 15.3 Termination

Either Party may terminate this MOU by providing [60] days' written notice, subject to:
- Settlement of all financial obligations
- Completion of ongoing activities or orderly transition
- Final reporting and CSR compliance

**Termination for Cause:**
- Material breach of MOU terms
- Fraud, corruption, or misappropriation of funds
- Failure to achieve minimum 50% of targets without valid reasons
- CACF violations or discrimination

### 15.4 Unutilized Funds

Any unutilized CSR funds shall be:
- Returned to Corporate Partner, OR
