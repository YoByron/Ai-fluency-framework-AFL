# AFL Pilot Program Implementation Guide
## Comprehensive 90-Day Proof of Concept Framework

**Version:** 1.0
**Alignment:** v8.1 Research Paper Phase 1 (2026)
**Document Type:** Implementation Guide

---

## Executive Summary

This guide provides detailed specifications for implementing the first phase of the AI Fluency Levels (AFL) Framework through structured pilot programs across 5 champion states. The pilot aims to:

- **Validate curriculum** across diverse demographic contexts
- **Establish assessment quality** standards and reliability
- **Measure economic impact** with rigorous methodology
- **Build partnership ecosystems** for scaled implementation
- **Generate 2M+ success stories** for national scaling

## 1. Pilot Overview

### 1.1 Scope Definition

| Parameter | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Duration** | 90 days (Phase 1a) + 90 days (Phase 1b) |
| **States** | 5 Champion States |
| **Target Certifications** | 2M AFL-0, 200K AFL-1, 20K AFL-2 |
| **Facilitators** | 20,000 (4,000 per state) |
| **Investment** | ₹1,000 Crore (Year 1) |

### 1.2 Pilot Objectives

**Primary Objectives:**
1. Validate curriculum effectiveness across diverse contexts
2. Establish facilitator training scalability
3. Measure productivity impact with statistical significance
4. Test technology platform at scale
5. Build institutional partnership models

**Secondary Objectives:**
1. Identify curriculum adaptation needs by state/language
2. Assess cost optimization opportunities
3. Generate public awareness and political support
4. Create replicable implementation playbooks
5. Establish quality assurance systems

### 1.3 Success Criteria

| Metric | Target | Threshold |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| **AFL-0 Certifications** | 2M | >1.5M |
| **AFL-1 Certifications** | 200K | >150K |
| **AFL-2 Certifications** | 20K | >15K |
| **Completion Rate** | 65% | >55% |
| **Learner Satisfaction** | 4.2/5 | >3.8/5 |
| **Facilitator NPS** | +50 | >+30 |
| **Productivity Gain (AFL-1)** | 15% | >10% |
| **Cost per Certification** | ₹400 | <₹500 |
| **CACF Compliance** | 95% | >90% |

## 2. State Selection Criteria

### 2.1 Selection Framework

**Mandatory Criteria (All must be met):**
1. State government written commitment
2. Existing digital infrastructure (DIKSHA, PMGDISHA presence)
3. Nodal officer designation within 15 days
4. 25% state co-funding commitment
5. Access to at least 3 language groups

**Weighted Scoring Criteria:**

| Criterion | Weight | Scoring Basis |
|-----------|--------|---------------|
| **Digital Infrastructure** | 25% | Internet penetration, smartphone ownership, existing platforms |
| **Government Capacity** | 20% | Administrative readiness, prior implementation success |
| **Demographic Diversity** | 20% | Urban-rural mix, linguistic diversity, SC/ST population |
| **Economic Potential** | 15% | MSME presence, knowledge worker population, growth trajectory |
| **Innovation Readiness** | 10% | Startup ecosystem, tech industry presence |
| **Geographic Balance** | 10% | Regional representation (N/S/E/W/Central) |

### 2.2 Selected Champion States

| State | Region | Primary Language | Key Strengths | Target Demographics |
|-------|--------|------------------|---------------|---------------------|
| **Karnataka** | South | Kannada, English | Tech hub, corporate partnerships, startup ecosystem | IT workers, students, MSMEs |
| **Kerala** | South | Malayalam | High literacy, rural reach, education infrastructure | Teachers, healthcare, women |
| **Uttar Pradesh** | North | Hindi | Scale demonstration, Hindi belt coverage, agricultural focus | Farmers, government officials, youth |
| **Tamil Nadu** | South | Tamil | Manufacturing focus, Dravidian language model, education | Industrial workers, students, MSMEs |
| **Madhya Pradesh** | Central | Hindi | Central India reach, tribal population, agricultural focus | Farmers, tribal communities, rural youth |

## 3. 90-Day Implementation Timeline

### Phase 1a: Days 1-30 (Foundation)

#### Week 1: State Activation

| Day | Activity | Responsible | Deliverable |
|-----|----------|-------------|-------------|
| 1-2 | State MoU signing | Central/State Leadership | Signed MoU |
| 2-3 | Nodal officer appointment | State Government | GO with designation |
| 3-5 | State unit team formation | Nodal Officer | Team roster |
| 5-7 | Infrastructure assessment | Tech Team | Gap analysis report |

#### Week 2: Content Localization

| Day | Activity | Responsible | Deliverable |
|-----|----------|-------------|-------------|
| 8-10 | Curriculum review for state context | Content Team | Adaptation requirements |
| 10-12 | Priority language selection | Content Team | Language priority list |
| 12-14 | Translation initiation | Content + Translation | Translation workflow |

#### Week 3: Platform Setup

| Day | Activity | Responsible | Deliverable |
|-----|----------|-------------|-------------|
| 15-17 | State portal configuration | Tech Team | Configured portal |
| 17-19 | Assessment system setup | Tech Team | Testing environment |
| 19-21 | Integration testing | Tech Team | Test report |

#### Week 4: Facilitator Recruitment

| Day | Activity | Responsible | Deliverable |
|-----|----------|-------------|-------------|
| 22-24 | Facilitator call announcement | Partnership Manager | Public notification |
| 24-28 | Application collection | District Coordinators | Application database |
| 28-30 | Initial screening | Facilitator Coordinators | Shortlist |

### Phase 1b: Days 31-60 (Preparation)

#### Week 5-6: Facilitator Training

| Day | Activity | Responsible | Deliverable |
|-----|----------|-------------|-------------|
| 31-35 | Master trainer training | Central Team | 200 master trainers |
| 35-45 | Cascade facilitator training | Master Trainers | 4,000 trained facilitators |

#### Week 7-8: Soft Launch

| Day | Activity | Responsible | Deliverable |
|-----|----------|-------------|-------------|
| 46-50 | Beta testing (500 learners) | Facilitators | Beta feedback |
| 50-55 | Curriculum refinement | Content Team | Updated curriculum |
| 55-60 | Technology optimization | Tech Team | Optimized platform |

### Phase 1c: Days 61-90 (Scaled Launch)

#### Week 9-10: Full Launch Preparation

| Day | Activity | Responsible | Deliverable |
|-----|----------|-------------|-------------|
| 61-65 | Public awareness campaign | Communications | Campaign materials |
| 65-70 | Registration opening | Tech Team | Registration portal live |
| 70-75 | Initial enrollments | Facilitators | 100K+ registrations |

#### Week 11-12: Full Operation

| Day | Activity | Responsible | Deliverable |
|-----|----------|-------------|-------------|
| 76-80 | AFL-0 delivery at scale | Facilitators | 500K+ enrollees |
| 80-85 | First certifications | Assessment Team | 100K+ certifications |
| 85-90 | Phase 1 evaluation | M&E Team | Impact report |

## 6. Facilitator Training Program

### 6.1 Facilitator Profile Requirements

**AFL-0 Facilitators:**
- Minimum education: Class 12 pass
- Age: 18-55 years
- Local language fluency: Required
- English: Basic reading (not mandatory)
- Digital comfort: Can operate smartphone

**AFL-1 Facilitators:**
- Minimum education: Graduate (any field)
- AI tool experience: 10+ hours demonstrated
- Digital skills: Proficient in online platforms
- Communication: Strong interpersonal skills

**AFL-2 Facilitators:**
- Minimum education: Post-graduate or professional degree
- AI implementation experience: Demonstrated in workplace
- Management experience: 2+ years preferred
- Training experience: Prior facilitation helpful

### 6.2 Training Curriculum

**AFL-0 Facilitator Training (40 hours)**

| Module | Duration | Content |
|--------|----------|---------|
| AFL Framework Overview | 4 hours | Philosophy, levels, CACF |
| AFL-0 Curriculum Mastery | 8 hours | Complete content understanding |
| Facilitation Skills | 8 hours | Adult learning, engagement techniques |
| Assessment Delivery | 6 hours | Quiz administration, evaluation |
| Technology Platform | 6 hours | Portal, apps, offline tools |
| Inclusion Practices | 4 hours | Gender, disability, language accessibility |
| Practicum | 4 hours | Observed facilitation session |

**AFL-1 Facilitator Training (80 hours)**

| Module | Duration | Content |
|--------|----------|---------|
| All AFL-0 content | 20 hours | Rapid review |
| AFL-1 Curriculum Mastery | 20 hours | Prompting, evaluation, ethics |
| Hands-on AI Practice | 15 hours | Cross-platform AI use |
| Portfolio Assessment | 10 hours | Project evaluation techniques |
| Advanced Facilitation | 10 hours | Workshop design, group dynamics |
| CACF Deep Dive | 5 hours | Bias detection, compliance |

### 6.3 Facilitator Incentive Structure

| Level | Base Compensation | Performance Bonus | Total Potential |
|-------|------------------|-------------------|-----------------|
| AFL-0 | ₹300/session | ₹50/certification (>70% rate) | ₹350-500/session |
| AFL-1 | ₹500/session | ₹100/certification (>75% rate) | ₹600-800/session |
| AFL-2 | ₹1,000/session | ₹200/certification (>80% rate) | ₹1,200-1,500/session |
| Master Trainer | ₹2,000/day | ₹5,000/trained facilitator batch | Variable |

## 10. Risk Management

### 10.1 Risk Register

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation | Contingency |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|-------------|
| **Low enrollment** | Medium | High | Aggressive awareness campaign, incentives | Extend timeline, additional marketing |
| **High dropout** | Medium | High | Gamification, facilitator follow-up | Peer support, curriculum simplification |
| **Technology failure** | Low | Critical | Redundancy, offline capability | SMS/paper backup |
| **Facilitator shortage** | Medium | High | Over-recruitment, flexible scheduling | Remote facilitation, video content |
| **Content quality issues** | Low | Medium | Review processes, pilot testing | Rapid iteration, expert input |
| **CACF non-compliance** | Low | High | Built-in compliance checks, audits | Immediate correction, retraining |
| **Political disruption** | Low | Critical | Multi-party consensus, IPR continuity | Central takeover, NGO delivery |
| **Budget constraints** | Medium | High | Conservative budgeting, reserves | Prioritization, phased rollback |

### 10.2 Escalation Protocol

| Level | Triggered By | Responder | Response Time |
|-------|--------------|-----------|---------------|
| **Green** | Normal operations | District Coordinator | Standard |
| **Yellow** | KPI 10% below target | State Nodal Officer | 48 hours |
| **Orange** | KPI 25% below target | Central Mission | 1 week |
| **Red** | Critical risk materialized | Ministry level | Immediate |
