 # Lucknow (Uttar Pradesh) — Urban Developing (AFL-0/1)
 
 ## Context
 - Cohort: 40,000 (AFL-0: 35k, AFL-1: 5k); high govt employee participation (~60% of AFL-1)
 
 ## Problem
 - Need to lift AI utilization among officials and urban citizens; strong Hindi preference
 
 ## Intervention
 - AFL-0 citywide awareness; AFL-1 for officials/teachers/MSMEs with Hindi-first content
 - Hybrid delivery (online + in-person)
 
 ## Implementation
 - UP-specific sector examples; DIKSHA + district training centers
 - Assessments: quizzes, practical tasks; portfolio of AI-assisted work
 - Modules referenced: curriculum/sectors/governance/modules/{policy_drafting.md,citizen_communications.md,file_noting_automation.md}
 
 ## Results
 - Completion: 61% (AFL-0), 42% (AFL-1)
 - Productivity: 12% avg for AFL-1 graduates
 - Cost/participant: ₹220 (AFL-0), ₹550 (AFL-1)
 
 ## CACF Compliance
 - Language fairness and regional equity checks passed; no critical flags
 
 ## Lessons Learned
 - Hindi-medium essential (~70% choose Hindi over English)
 - Hybrid delivery outperforms single-mode
 
 ## Next Steps
 - Expand Hindi sector modules; target more departments for AFL-2 pilots
