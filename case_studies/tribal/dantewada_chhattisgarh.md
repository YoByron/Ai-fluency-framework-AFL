 # Dantewada (Chhattisgarh) — Tribal Area (AFL-0)
 
 ## Context
 - Cohort: 15,000 (AFL-0 only); low device availability; trust-building required
 
 ## Problem
 - Limited access and trust; heavy facilitator dependence; tribal language needs
 
 ## Intervention
 - AFL-0 via community facilitation; elders as champions; offline-first methods
 
 ## Implementation
 - Facilitators ~1:30; device sharing; culturally adapted content
 
 ## Results
 - Completion: 42%; Cost/participant: ₹350
 
 ## CACF Compliance
 - Accessibility and inclusion prioritized; audits logged
 
 ## Lessons Learned
 - Trust-building phase critical; language/culture adaptation non-negotiable
 
 ## Next Steps
 - Device-lending; pathway to AFL-1 with voice/SMS support
