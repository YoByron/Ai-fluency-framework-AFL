 # Thrissur (Kerala) — Agriculture/Education Pilot (AFL-0/1)
 
 ## Context
 - Rural-progressive district; period: 2024–2025; Cohort: 30,000 (AFL-0: 25k, AFL-1: 5k)
 
 ## Problem
 - Need for farmer advisory and teacher workload relief in low-capex settings
 
 ## Intervention (AFL Levels)
 - AFL-0 for general awareness
 - AFL-1 for teachers and farmers (vernacular, sector examples)
 
 ## Implementation
 - Community-center delivery; Malayalam content accelerated learning
 - Strong teacher participation (~40%)
 - Agriculture pathway: weather, crop planning, pest identification
 - Modules referenced: curriculum/sectors/agriculture/modules/{advisories.md,pest_identification.md,market_and_negotiation.md}
 
 ## Results
 - Completion: 68% (AFL-0), 51% (AFL-1)
 - Productivity: 15% avg for AFL-1 graduates
 - Cost/participant: ₹200 (AFL-0), ₹480 (AFL-1)
 
 ## CACF Compliance
 - Bias checks focused on language/regional equity; no critical issues
 
 ## Lessons Learned
 - Community centers highly effective
 - Sector-specific, vernacular content drives outcomes
 
 ## Next Steps
 - Expand to agriculture cooperatives; add offline-first modules
