# AFL-2 Assessment Rubric – AI Integration

## Competencies
- Workflow design and integration
- Bias detection and CACF compliance
- Impact measurement and reporting
- Train-the-trainer capability

## Instruments
1. Integration Project – ≥20% efficiency gain (before/after evidence)
2. CACF Audit – Equality ≥0.95, Bias ≤0.05, Explainability ≥0.80, no critical flags
3. Report – KPIs, mitigations, lessons; peer review pass
4. T3 Demo – Deliver a 30–60 min micro-session on AFL-0/1

## KPIs
- 60% of graduates deploy at least one AI-enhanced workflow within 60 days
