# AFL-3 Assessment Rubric – AI Orchestration

## Competencies
- Multi-agent/system architecture
- Program strategy and ROI
- Governance at scale (CACF)
- Change leadership

## Instruments
1. Capstone: Enterprise orchestration plan (≥30% target efficiency; feasibility reviewed)
2. Governance Pack: CACF policy, audit plan, risk register
3. Enablement Playbook: Capability ladder and rollout plan

## KPIs
- 70% implement pilots within 90 days; 30% reach scale in 6–12 months
