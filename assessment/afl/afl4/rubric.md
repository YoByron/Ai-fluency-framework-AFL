# AFL-4 Assessment Rubric – AI Strategy & Leadership

## Competencies
- Enterprise/sector strategy
- Policy/regulatory design
- Ecosystem building
- Strategic CACF oversight

## Instruments
1. Strategy Dossier (3–5 year plan with KPIs/budgets)
2. Policy/Regulation Proposal with stakeholder map
3. Partnership & Sustainability Blueprint

## KPIs
- Demonstrated org-level transformation program initiated within 6 months
