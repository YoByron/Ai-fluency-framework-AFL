 # Longitudinal Evaluation Protocol (AI Fluency Development)

Goals: Track competency growth, transfer to work/study, and retention over 24–36 months.

- Cohorts: per tier entry; baseline, post, 6, 12, 24, 36 months
- Measures: tier scores, subscales, portfolio ratings, workplace/academic KPIs, self‑efficacy
- Design: mixed‑methods; repeated measures + qualitative interviews (n≈30/site)
- Analysis: growth curves (LMM), effect sizes, subgroup equity gaps, mediation (practice → outcomes)
- Data: consent, privacy, PII minimization, de‑identification; secure storage; DPA compliance
- Attrition: incentives, multi‑channel follow‑ups, imputation where appropriate
- Reporting: periodic briefs; public summary with safeguards
