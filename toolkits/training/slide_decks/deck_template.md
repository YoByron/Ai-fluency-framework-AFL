 # Slide Deck Template (Facilitator)

Use this outline to prepare concise, local-language friendly decks.

1) Title and Objectives (1 slide)
2) Why AI Fluency now (1–2 slides)
3) Today’s Outcomes (bullets)
4) Core Content (3–6 slides; reference modules/activities)
5) Practice/Drills (1–2 slides)
6) Assessment/Next Steps (1 slide)
7) Localization Tips (appendix)

Notes:
- Link to sector modules and assessments rather than duplicating content.
- Include CACF reminders on privacy/fairness where relevant.
