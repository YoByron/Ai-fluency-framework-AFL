 # Government Deployment Playbook

## Phases
- Phase 1: AFL-0 awareness for citizens; AFL-1 for officials
- Phase 2: AFL-2 workflow projects (grievances, drafting, dashboards)
- Phase 3: AFL-3 orchestration; CACF governance rollout

## Metrics
- Pendency reduction, service turnaround, citizen satisfaction
- Equity: participation by gender/caste/language/region
- CACF: equality ≥0.95, bias ≤0.05, no critical flags
