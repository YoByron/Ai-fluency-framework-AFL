# AFL-4: AI Strategy & Leadership

## Objective
Equip top leaders to set AI vision, design policy, build ecosystems, and embed CACF into strategic decision-making.

## Core Competencies
By the end of AFL-4, learners will be able to:
1. Formulate enterprise or sector-wide AI strategy and govern a portfolio of AI initiatives.
2. Design effective AI policy and regulatory frameworks, engaging with the public and stakeholders.
3. Build and sustain innovation ecosystems that bring together industry, academia, and government.
4. Provide strategic oversight for the Constitutional AI Compliance Framework (CACF) and ensure accountability.
