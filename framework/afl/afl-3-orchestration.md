# AFL-3: AI Orchestration

## Objective
Prepare leaders to architect multi-agent/multi-tool systems, govern enterprise-wide AI adoption, and institutionalize CACF compliance.

## Core Competencies
By the end of AFL-3, learners will be able to:
1. Design and architect multi-agent/multi-tool AI systems and integration patterns.
2. Develop program-level AI strategy, ROI models, and implementation roadmaps.
3. Institute governance at scale, including risk management, CACF audits, and grievance procedures.
4. Lead organizational change and build internal AI capability.
