# AFL-5: AI Thought Leadership & Societal Impact

## Objective
Develop leaders who advance research, shape global governance, and drive large-scale societal impact anchored in constitutional values.

## Core Competencies
By the end of AFL-5, learners will be able to:
1. Conduct original research and develop new frameworks for AI adoption and governance.
2. Shape global governance standards and engage in AI-related diplomacy.
3. Lead large-scale societal transformation projects that address "grand challenges" using AI.
4. Drive innovation in constitutional AI, developing extensions and new applications of the CACF.
