# AFL-2: AI Integration

## Objective
Enable practitioners to design and integrate AI into real workflows, delivering measured productivity gains while embedding CACF compliance.

## Core Competencies
By the end of AFL-2, learners will be able to:
1. Analyze workflows and identify opportunities for AI integration.
2. Implement multi-tool AI integrations and optimize processes.
3. Detect bias in AI systems and implement CACF compliance measures.
4. Measure the impact of AI integration and report on key performance indicators.
5. Teach foundational AI concepts to others (Train-the-Trainer for AFL-0/1).
